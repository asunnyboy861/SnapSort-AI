Frequently Asked Questions

How does SnapSort AI work?

SnapSort AI automatically detects new screenshots on your device and uses on-device AI to categorize them into 13 smart categories (OTP codes, receipts, recipes, etc.). It also performs OCR text recognition so you can search through your screenshots by their text content.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely! All processing happens 100% on your device using Apple's Vision framework. No data is ever uploaded to any server. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Does it require internet?

No! All core features work completely offline. OCR, classification, and search all run on-device.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

The free version includes auto-detection, basic categorization (up to 100 screenshots), OCR (50/month), and search. Pro ($2.99 one-time) unlocks unlimited everything plus custom tags, Face ID lock, widgets, batch operations, iCloud sync, Siri shortcuts, and more.

How does auto-clean work?

Temporary screenshots (OTP codes, QR codes) are automatically flagged for cleanup after 24 hours. You'll receive a notification before any deletion, and you can always save favorites to prevent auto-deletion.

Why can't I see my screenshots?

Make sure you've granted Photo Library access. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos > SnapSort AI and ensure access is set to "All Photos".

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Your Pro status will be restored from your Apple ID.
