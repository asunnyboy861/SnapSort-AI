Privacy Policy for SnapSort AI

Last updated: May 4, 2026

1. Data Collection

SnapSort AI is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect NO personal data from you.

  • No server uploads: All OCR text recognition and image classification happens 100% on your device using Apple's Vision framework.
  • No analytics: We do not collect usage analytics, crash reports, or any tracking data.
  • No accounts: No sign-up required. No email, no phone number, no personal information needed.
  • No third-party sharing: Since we collect nothing, we share nothing.

2. Photo Library Access

SnapSort AI requests access to your photo library solely to:

  • Detect new screenshots
  • Read screenshot images for OCR text recognition and classification
  • Delete temporary screenshots (with your permission)

Your photos are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared. All processing is done locally on your device.

3. On-Device Processing

All AI features run entirely on your device:

  • OCR text recognition: Apple Vision Framework (on-device)
  • Image classification: Apple Vision Framework (on-device)
  • QR code detection: Apple Vision Framework (on-device)

No internet connection is required for any core feature.

4. Data Storage

App data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData:

  • Screenshot metadata (category, OCR text, tags)
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable iCloud sync (Pro feature, Phase 2).

5. In-App Purchases

Pro upgrade is processed through Apple's StoreKit. We only receive a transaction receipt from Apple — no personal information is shared with us.

6. Children's Privacy

SnapSort AI does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, our app is safe for all ages.

7. Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to delete, export, or modify. All your data stays on your device under your full control.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
